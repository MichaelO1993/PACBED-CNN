PACBED CNN

Select a DM4 file with a PACBED pattern and specify the metadata. By clicking "Submit", the file is uploaded and a pre-trained neuronal networks derives thickness and mistilt. Finally, rhe result and validation information are displayed.

Pre-trained models are available for Rutile and Strontium titanate along the <0 0 1> zone axis with and acceleration voltage of 80 kV and a convergence angle of 20 mrad for this demonstration.
